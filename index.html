The Norbeks history

Norbeks was founded by Marina in the name of her grandfather Norbek; a veteran of World War II who had gone above and beyond to provide a brighter future for his granddaughter.

Norbeks is a manifestation of Marina's inspiring journey from a hard-working youth in Uzbekistan to a courageous, educated and highly experienced woman in the UK today.

We are committed to carve a better present for you.
